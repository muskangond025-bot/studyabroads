Design a modern “How It Works” section for an Event Management website landing page.

Frame & Layout:

Create a desktop frame (1440px width)

Section width: max 1100px, center aligned

Vertical padding: 100px top and bottom

Background: very light neutral (#F8FAFC or similar)

Section Header:

Title: “How It Works”

Font size: 36px, semi-bold

Center aligned

Subtitle (below title):

Text: “Plan and manage your events in a few simple steps”

Font size: 16px, regular, muted gray

Spacing: 12px below title

Timeline Structure:

Create a vertical center line (2px width, light gray #E2E8F0)

Place 5 steps along the vertical line

Equal vertical spacing between steps (80–100px)

Step Layout (alternating left-right):

Step 1: content on left, Step 2: right, alternating pattern

Each step includes:

Circular node on the timeline:

Size: 16px

Fill: primary color (e.g. #4F46E5)

Card container:

Width: 420px

Padding: 20px

Background: white

Border radius: 12px

Shadow: soft (0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.05))

Card Content (each step):

Step number (small label): “Step 01”, “Step 02”, etc.

Font: 12px, medium, uppercase, gray

Title:

Font size: 18px, semi-bold

Description:

Font size: 14px, regular, gray

Max 2 lines

Example Steps Content:

Discover Events – “Browse and explore events tailored to your needs”

Book Services – “Select venues, vendors, and packages بسهولة”

Plan Details – “Customize schedule, guests, and requirements”

Execute Event – “Manage everything smoothly on event day”

Enjoy & Review – “Experience your event and share feedback”

Visual Style:

Primary color: Indigo or Purple (#4F46E5)

Text color: #0F172A (dark), secondary #64748B

Font: Inter or similar modern sans-serif

Consistent spacing system (8px grid)

Responsive Behavior:

On mobile (375px):

Timeline shifts to left side

All cards align to right side (no alternating)

Reduce card width to 100%

Do:

Keep spacing consistent and clean

Maintain clear visual hierarchy

Use subtle shadows and soft colors

Ensure timeline line is perfectly centered

Don’t:

Do not overcrowd cards with text

Avoid heavy shadows or dark backgrounds

Do not misalign nodes and cards

Avoid inconsistent spacing between steps
